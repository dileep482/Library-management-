❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my membership plan anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your membership plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the beginning of your next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and digital wallets. Your payment is secure and encrypted.
Is there a cancellation fee?
No! You can cancel your membership at any time without any cancellation fees. Access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle.
What happens when I reach my book limit?
Once you reach your book limit (based on your membership tier), you'll need to return one of your books before borrowing another one.
Do you offer student or senior discounts?
Yes! We offer 25% off for students and 20% off for seniors. Please verify your eligibility in our support center.
What's included in the monthly vs. annual plans?
The features are identical! Annual plans simply offer better value with a 17% discount compared to paying monthly. Choose based on your preference and commitment level.
